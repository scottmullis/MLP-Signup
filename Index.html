About the Event

Major League Pickleball returns to Chaifetz Arena for the 3rd stop of the 2026 Regular Season, and our hometown St. Louis Shock — fresh off their 2025 MLP Cup title — will defend their home court against a select lineup of MLP's elite Premier Level teams. Expect nonstop co-ed team competition across four courts, with the biggest matches on Championship Court.

Days 1–3 feature group play, with every team facing every other team in their group. Day 4 (Sunday) is the showdown round, where seeds are matched head-to-head and standings points are on the line. Admission includes access to all four match courts, the bar, concessions, DJ, sponsor tents, and more. VIP Lounge upgrades also available.
